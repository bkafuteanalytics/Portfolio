RTSA ACCIDENT ANALYSIS

Using Data to Drive Safety Improvements

Scenario

Road accidents are a major public health issue, As part of your role, analyse the RTSA accident data to identify patterns and trends that can inform safety improvements and policy decisions. Your analysis should include visualizations and recommendations for reducing accidents and improving road safety.

Project Summary

I built a dashboard that combines accident severity breakdowns, casualty trends by vehicle types involved, road type & conditions, and time-series accident patterns. Locaion-based accident hotspots are visualized for quick identification of high-risk areas. and filters making it easy to explore specific factors.

Technologies Used

Excel, SQL

Key Challenges

Cleaning and preparing the data for analysis, dealing with missing or inconsistent data, and identifying relevant factors that contribute to accidents.

Solutions

Data cleaning and preprocessing techniques were used to ensure data quality. Exploratory data analysis (EDA) was conducted to identify patterns and trends in the data. Visualizations were created using Excel to effectively communicate findings and recommendations.

What I Learned

This project helped me to develop my skills in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and data visualization. I also gained a deeper understanding of the factors that contribute to road accidents and the importance of using data to inform safety improvements and policy decisions.

Why It Matters

This project is important because it addresses a major public health issue and has the potential to save lives by identifying patterns and trends that informed safety improvements and policy decisions. By using data to drive decision-making, we can create safer roads and reduce the number of accidents and casualties.
